I CANNOT DONATE BLOOD
No problem! You can use donation receipts from family or friends. If that is not possible, just helping spread the campaign is already a great contribution.
IS THE RECEIPT REQUIRED?
To register your donation in the campaign, attaching the receipt (IMAGE/PDF) on the website is mandatory.
SECURITY AND RELIABILITY
All data is confidential and used only for campaign purposes.
DONATIONS MADE BEFORE THE CAMPAIGN
If you made your donation before the campaign started but during 2026, it will still count for Doa Kingos 2026.
OTHER QUESTIONS
