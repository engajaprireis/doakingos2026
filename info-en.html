I CAN'T DONATE BLOOD
No problem! You can use the receipt from family members or friends who have donated. If this is not possible, sharing the campaign is already a great help.

To register your donation in the campaign, attaching the receipt (IMAGE/PDF) on the website is mandatory.
SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
All data is confidential and used only for campaign purposes.
DONATIONS MADE BEFORE THE CAMPAIGN
If you made your donation before the start of the campaign but during 2026, it will also be valid for Doa Kingos 2026.
