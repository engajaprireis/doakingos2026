HOW IT WORKS

1. Make your donation

Go to a blood center or blood bank in your city and donate normally.

2. Keep your receipt

After donating, save or photograph the donation receipt provided by the center.

3. Register on the website

On the homepage, click "Register my donation", fill out the form, and attach your receipt.

4. Your donation will be counted

After verification, your donation will be added to the official campaign counter.
