HOW IT WORKS

1. Make your donation

Go to a blood center or blood bank in your city and complete your donation as usual.

2. Keep your receipt

After donating, save or take a photo of the donation receipt provided by the location.

3. Register on the website

On the homepage, click “Register my donation”, fill out the form and attach your receipt.

4. Your donation will be counted

After verification, your donation will be added to our official campaign counter.
